Is NATO Scared?

Following the Russian invasion in Ukraine, there has been a substantial increase in the defense expenditures of NATO nations, particularly those close to Russia, hoping to not suffer the same fate as Ukraine. Russia's actions in both 2014 and 2022 have also triggered many NATO countries to now attempt to meet the 2% guideline for defense spending that all NATO countries pledged to meet in 2006 and again in 2014.

This "scrolly-telling" piece, featuring static web maps, was a project for the Graphic Design in Cartography course at UW-Madison. I leveraged appropriate cartographic visual variables to accompany a narrative about the increase in defense spending throughout Europe following Russia's invasion of Ukraine. The maps and content were all created within ArcGIS StoryMaps.
